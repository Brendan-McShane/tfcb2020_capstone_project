The correlations between these continuous variables show that RNFL4.mean and mean_deviation are the most well correlated, with a Pearson correlation coefficient of ~0.679. The visualization above displays the correlation coefficients using both a red-blue color scale and by the size of the square within the box. I trimmed off the mirroring of the values, self-pairings, andchanged the font color to black for better readability.
